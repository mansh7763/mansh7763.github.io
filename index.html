The Geometry of Entanglement: Bridging Representation Probing and Mechanistic Interpretability

Himanshu Kumar (Primary Researcher)

Multi-scale investigation of implicit attribute encoding in deep vision models, revealing fundamental asymmetry between task-relevant and implicitly encoded features through geometric analysis.

Discovered task features use localized strong correlations (~750 neurons, |r|>0.6)
Implicit attributes use distributed weak signals (~1,200 neurons, |r|<0.2) with 94% separability
95% of gender info concentrates in 1/16 of representation dimensions (ResNet-50)
Removing bias-carrying components destroys 40% task accuracy (causal entanglement)
